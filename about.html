Who We Are, What We Do



Local Edge Farm is an experiment in how food is grown. We aim to keep food local, and growing in a regenerative system ensures it's safe, wholesome, and higher quality of food to eat. While we are mastering the growing of food, we are also passionate about creating food, particularly with our pizza catering.
