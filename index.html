Regenerative Produce Farm

Central City, NE





Tucked just outside small-town Central City, Local Edge provides a different take to how your food is grown and consumed. Combatting the current food system, Local Edge focues on high quality, localized produce that enriches the environmental and economical impacts regionally. Produce that is grown in a sustainable, regenerative system will contribute to the overall health of a community that can't be supplemented through food that doesn't have a local footprint.
